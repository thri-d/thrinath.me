Second-year PhD student in Educational Psychology at the University of Arizona, where I also serve as a Graduate Teaching Assistant. My research examines how social belonging shapes physiological wellbeing, specifically whether sense of belonging predicts diurnal cortisol slope in college students, moderated by other important factors.
